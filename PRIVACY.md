# Privacy Policy

**Bubbly-fi Laundry Shop**
92 14th Ave, Cubao, Quezon City, 1109 Metro Manila, Philippines
Effective date: July 10, 2026

This policy explains what the Bubbly-fi Laundry app collects and how it is used. If you have questions, message us at [facebook.com/bubblyfi](https://www.facebook.com/bubblyfi) or call +63 998 885 5650.

## Information we collect

- **Booking details you enter**: laundry type, quantity, drop-off/pickup time, delivery address, notes, and chosen payment method (Cash, GCash, Maya, or Bank Transfer).
- **Location, only if you choose delivery**: with your permission, the app can use your device's GPS to auto-fill your delivery address. This happens on your device; we do not track your location in the background or at any other time.
- We do **not** collect payment card numbers or process payments inside the app. Payment (Cash, GCash, Maya, Bank Transfer) is settled directly with the shop, outside the app.

## How we use it

Booking details and delivery location are used solely to prepare, price, and fulfill your laundry order. We do not sell your information, use it for advertising, or share it with third parties other than as needed to fulfill your order (for example, a delivery rider seeing your address).

## Data retention

Order details are kept only as long as needed for order fulfillment and basic business recordkeeping.

## Your choices

- Location access is optional and only requested when you choose a delivery option. You can decline it and enter your address manually, or deny/revoke the permission at any time in your device settings.
- You can contact us at any time to ask what information we hold about you or to request it be deleted.

## Children's privacy

This app is not directed at children and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the effective date above will be updated.
