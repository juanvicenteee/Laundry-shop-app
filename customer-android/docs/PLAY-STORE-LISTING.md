# Play Store listing copy

## Bubbly-fi Operations

**App name:** Bubbly-fi Operations

**Short description:** Manage laundry orders, requests, inventory, payments and daily operations.

**Full description:**
Bubbly-fi Operations is the staff application for Bubbly-fi Laundry Shop. Authorized Admin and Operator users can receive customer requests, create and update orders, manage laundry workflow statuses, monitor payments, review inventory consumption, view customer records, and analyze sales performance. The application connects securely to the existing Bubbly-fi Supabase cloud database so authorized devices share current operational data.

## Bubbly-fi Laundry

**App name:** Bubbly-fi Laundry

**Short description:** Book laundry pickup, choose services, pay via GCash and submit your request.

**Full description:**
Book Bubbly-fi Laundry Shop services from your phone. Choose a service and laundry type, enter the estimated weight, select detergent, fabric conditioner and add-ons, provide your pickup address, optionally share GPS and photos, and submit GCash payment proof. The app displays the calculated service total and clearly identifies LalaMove delivery charges that are quoted separately for locations outside Cubao. Final billable weight is verified using the shop weighing scale before processing.
