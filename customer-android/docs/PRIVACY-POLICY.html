Bubbly-fi Laundry Shop collects information necessary to receive, schedule, process and deliver laundry-service requests.

Information collected

This may include your name, telephone number, pickup address, optional precise location, selected services, estimated laundry weight, pickup schedule, notes, optional pickup and item photos, GCash reference, and payment proof.

How information is used

We use the information to calculate and confirm bookings, arrange pickup and delivery, verify payment, communicate order status, prevent abuse, resolve disputes, and maintain legally or operationally necessary business records.

Storage and security

Information is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections and stored using cloud service providers. Sensitive images should be stored in private storage and exposed to authorized staff only through short-lived access links.

Retention

Payment proof is removed after reconciliation according to shop policy. Pickup and laundry photos and precise GPS coordinates are removed after operational and dispute periods. Rejected or abandoned requests are purged according to configured retention schedules. Completed business records may be retained while unnecessary images and location details are deleted.

Sharing

Data may be processed by service providers used for cloud storage, authentication, SMS, email, maps and delivery coordination. Bubbly-fi does not sell customer personal information.

Your choices

GPS and pickup/laundry photos are optional. Contact Bubbly-fi to request access, correction or deletion where applicable.
